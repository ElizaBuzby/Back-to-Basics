Arcane

Arcane is a popular animated show created by netflix. The show is well known for it's distinctive art style, and compelling story and characters. Along with this, the music for the show is masterful, resulting in it being my favorite show.


Madoka Magica

Madoka Magica is an popular magical girl anime that reinvented the mahou shoujo scene. The anime has a detailed and dark story, and beautiful sapphic story. On top of all this, the soundtrack is fantastic, resulting in Madoka Magica being another one of my favorite shows.


Star Wars: The Clone Wars

Star Wars: The Clone Wars is an 7 season 3D animated show that details the events of the Clone Wars in the Star Wars universe. The series has incredible character writing and dialogue, resulting in being another one of my favorite shows.


Lego Monkie Kid

Starting out as a cute 2D animated show, the show quickly gains gravitas with it's well-written characters and beautiful group dynamics. With all this, it earns it's spot as one of my favorite shows of all time.
